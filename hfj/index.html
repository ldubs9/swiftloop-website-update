Atelier / who we are
Shop № 2 — Al Buteen Bldg.

In the lanes of Deira's Gold Souq, where the world comes to trade in brilliance, our family workshop turns rough crystal into stones of consequence.

Every commission is handled by hand — sourced from trusted mines and lapidaries, examined under loupe, then cut, calibrated and polished to the exact dimensions your setting demands. Whether it's a single museum-grade sapphire or a thousand calibrated onyx cabochons, the standard is the same.

  • Natural precious & semi-precious stones
  • Calibrated sizes, cut to order
  • Beads & strands, matched by hand
  • Synthetic & lab-grown on request

The four hands
of a stone

Craft / how a commission moves
I — Source

Select

Rough and pre-forms are sourced from mines and cutters we've dealt with for years — then graded by eye and loupe at the bench.

II — Cut

Facet

Each stone is oriented for colour, then cut and faceted to your drawing — standard shapes or fantasy cuts, to the tenth of a millimetre.

III — Calibrate

Match

Production lots are calibrated and colour-matched piece by piece, so every stone drops into its setting without adjustment.

IV — Deliver

Dispatch

Finished goods are checked, papered and shipped worldwide — or collected over coffee at Shop № 2, the way the souq prefers.
