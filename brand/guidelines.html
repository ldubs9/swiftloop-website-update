The complete identity system for SwiftLoop — a web-design and AI-automation studio in Dubai. This document governs every surface the brand touches: web, print, proposals, invoices and product UI.

Brand core

01 / Foundation

Kinetic precision. SwiftLoop sits at the intersection of hand-crafted design and machine-run systems. The identity expresses that duality everywhere: ink canvas, bone type, one signal-orange accent, and monospaced telemetry. Designed by hand, run by machine.

Positioning

Award-calibre sites, self-running systems

We build websites people remember and wire them to AI workflows that never sleep. Premium craft, attached to a retainer by default — the system compounds.

Personality

Precise. Confident. Quietly technical.

Never loud, never cheap. The brand speaks like an engineer who designs — short declarative lines, real numbers, no filler adjectives.

The loop

Map → Design → Automate → Iterate

The four-phase process is a brand asset. The circle / ring motif in the logo mark is the loop made visible — closed, continuous, compounding.

Logo system

02 / Identity marks

The identity has two components: the wordmark (primary) and the loop ring (symbol). The wordmark is a single Clash Display Semibold setting — "Swift" in bone, "Loop" and the terminal period in signal orange. The period is non-negotiable: it is the full stop of a confident sentence.

Construction rules
● DOKeep clear space of at least the cap-height of the "S" on all sides.
● DOMinimum size — wordmark: 90 px / 24 mm wide. Ring: 16 px / 5 mm.
● DORing stroke weight is 14 % of the viewbox (14 units on a 100-unit grid). Never thin it.
✕ DON'TRecolour "Swift", change the orange, add gradients, shadows or outlines.
✕ DON'TSet the wordmark in any typeface other than Clash Display Semibold.
✕ DON'TDrop the terminal period, stretch, rotate, or place on busy photography without a scrim.

Colour

03 / Palette

The palette is deliberately narrow: three inks, two bones, one signal. Signal Orange is the only saturated colour in the system — it marks action, motion and the loop itself. If everything is orange, nothing is. Target ratio on any layout: ~80 % ink, ~15 % bone, ~5 % orange.

Graphic language

05 / Texture & motion
Hairlines

1 px rules everywhere

Sections, tables and cards are separated by 1 px hairlines at 12 % bone — never solid boxes or drop shadows. The grid shows itself.

Grain

5 % film grain

A fractal-noise grain overlay at 5 % opacity sits on top of digital surfaces. It keeps the dark canvas analogue and alive.

Pills

Fully-rounded chips

Buttons and tags use 99 px radius pills with mono uppercase labels. Primary = bone fill; ghost = 1 px line; hover floods orange.

Telemetry

Coordinates & counters

Real data as decoration: latitudes, timestamps, phase numbers, percentages. The brand proves it measures things.

Motion

Masked reveals

Type enters through line-masks; eases use cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1). Motion is swift and decisive — no bounces, no floaty drift.

The ring

Circular geometry

Circles recur: the ring mark, round close buttons, the "◦" separator, radial orange glows behind CTAs. Every circle is the loop.

Voice & tone

06 / Language

Write like the type is set: short, declarative, kinetic. Claims come with numbers. The em-dash and the period do the heavy lifting — exclamation marks never appear.

● We say

"Ship in weeks, not quarters."
"Designed by hand, run by machine."
"Tell us what eats your team's week. We'll show you the system that gives it back."

✕ We never say

"Affordable websites for every budget!"
"We're passionate about leveraging cutting-edge synergies."
"Cheap, fast and reliable!!!"

● DOLead with the outcome, attach the mechanism: "3.4× average conversion lift — measured, not promised."
● DOPremium posture in commercial documents: builds are quoted at AED 4,000–7,000+, always with a care retainer attached by default. Removing the retainer is presented as a downgrade, never a discount.
● DOReferral language: every client is told plainly — one month of retainer free for any introduction that closes.
✕ DON'TUse "cheap", "budget", "basic" or apologise for pricing. SwiftLoop serves luxury and premium clients; the language must match.
