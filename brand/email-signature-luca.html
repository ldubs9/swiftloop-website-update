Install

  1. Select Copy signature.
  2. Open your email app’s signature settings and paste it into a new signature.
  3. Send yourself a test email and confirm the logo and both links work.

The approved wordmark is loaded from a pinned public GitHub asset so it works immediately. Keep image loading enabled when testing.
