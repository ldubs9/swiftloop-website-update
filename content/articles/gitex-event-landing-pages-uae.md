# High-Converting Event & GITEX Landing Pages for UAE Companies

GITEX, LEAP, the countless conferences and expos the UAE hosts — exhibiting is a serious investment of money and effort. Yet most companies point all that attention at their generic homepage, where the specific reason someone scanned their badge gets lost. A dedicated landing page captures the moment, and the leads, far better.

## Why your homepage is the wrong target

Your homepage is built for everyone — every service, every audience. A visitor who just met you at GITEX has a specific context and a short attention span. Sending them to a sprawling homepage forces them to hunt for the thing they cared about. They won't. A focused landing page meets them exactly where the conversation left off.

## What a winning event page does

- **One clear message and one action.** Mirror the promise from your stand or talk, and make the next step — book a demo, download the deck, claim the offer — unmistakable.
- **Speak to the event audience.** Reference the event and the specific value for *them*. Specificity converts; generic doesn't.
- **Capture the lead cleanly.** A short form or an instant WhatsApp path, feeding straight into your CRM so follow-up starts immediately.
- **Move fast.** A laggy page loses a distracted, on-the-move conference visitor in seconds.

## Use a clean, measurable URL

Give the page its own memorable URL (and a QR code for your stand and slides). It's easy to share, easy to type, and lets you measure exactly what the event drove — which leads came from where, and what they did. That's how you prove the ROI of exhibiting.

## Follow up before the badge cools

Event leads decay fast — everyone's collecting the same business cards. The page should trigger instant, automated follow-up: a thank-you, the promised resource, and a nudge toward a meeting, while you're still fresh in their memory. By the time competitors get around to emailing, you've already booked the call.

## After the event, repurpose it

A good event page isn't disposable. Update it into an evergreen campaign page, or archive the learnings for the next expo. The structure — focused message, single action, fast capture — works for any campaign, not just GITEX.

## The takeaway

If you're investing in a UAE event, invest in a dedicated landing page to catch what it generates: one message, one action, fast capture, and instant follow-up on a measurable URL. We build event and campaign pages that turn badge scans into booked meetings.
