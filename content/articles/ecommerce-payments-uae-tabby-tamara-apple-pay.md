# Setting Up Payments for a UAE Online Store

You can have the most beautiful storefront in Dubai and still lose the sale at the last screen. Checkout is where intent turns into revenue — or doesn't. In the UAE, that final step has its own rules, and stores built for elsewhere quietly leak money here.

## Offer the methods UAE shoppers actually use

A UAE checkout that only takes a Visa form is behind the market. Shoppers here expect:

- **Apple Pay and Google Pay** — adoption is high, and one-tap pay dramatically lifts mobile conversion.
- **Buy-now-pay-later (Tabby, Tamara)** — now standard, especially for higher-ticket and fashion. Split payments measurably raise average order value, which matters for premium goods.
- **Local and international cards** — via a gateway that settles in AED.
- **Cash on delivery** — still expected by a meaningful slice of shoppers. Love it or not, removing it costs you orders.

## Choose the right gateway

Options like Telr, PayTabs, Stripe, Checkout.com and Network's N-Genius all operate here. The right one depends on settlement speed, fees, AED support, and which wallets and BNPL providers it bundles. The wrong one means awkward integrations and a checkout that feels bolted-on. We pick and wire the gateway that fits your product and margins.

## Build for trust and Arabic

A high-value buyer abandons a checkout that feels unsafe. Show security cues plainly, keep the flow short, and make it work flawlessly in **Arabic and English** — a mirrored, native checkout is part of serving the UAE properly, not an afterthought.

## Remove every gram of friction

The data is unforgiving: each extra field and forced account loses buyers. Guest checkout, autofill, saved methods, and a single clear "pay" action beat a long form every time. For mobile — where most UAE traffic lives — this is the whole game.

## Don't forget VAT and compliance

UAE e-commerce carries 5% VAT and FTA-compliant invoicing obligations. A proper build calculates VAT correctly at checkout and issues compliant receipts automatically, so your books stay clean as you scale.

## The takeaway

In the UAE, checkout isn't plumbing — it's conversion. Offer the wallets and BNPL shoppers expect, settle in AED, work in Arabic, and strip the friction. We build UAE-ready checkouts that turn the last click into a sale.
