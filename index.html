FAQ / web design in Dubai

How much does a website cost in Dubai?

Most builds land between AED 4,000 and 7,000 — it comes down to how many pages you need and how much we're automating behind them. Want it handled for the long haul? We also run monthly retainers that keep the design sharp and the systems looping.

How do you build a website for a business?

Four phases, one loop. We map your funnel and your busywork, design it live in the browser, automate the work that runs behind it, then iterate every month as the data comes in. Built on a modern stack so it stays fast — and never stops improving.

Do you build luxury and jewelry websites?

Often — jewelry and luxury brands are some of our favourite work. Pieces like Velora Fine Jewelry and Falcon Timepieces are usually bilingual (English / Arabic), with AI quietly keeping the inventory in check.

Where is SwiftLoop based?

We're a Dubai studio, working across the UAE — and remotely with brands just about anywhere. Designed by hand here, running on autopilot wherever you are.
